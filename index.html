From Prototypes to Progress: Advancing Innovation through Additive Manufacturing and AI

October 22-23, 2025 | Solaire Resort, Paranaque, Metro Manila

Learn More

The Asian Conference on Advance Manufacturing is the region’s first major event dedicated to additive manufacturing. With the theme “From Prototypes to Progress: Advancing Innovation with Additive Manufacturing,” it brings together experts, innovators, researchers, and industry leaders to showcase breakthroughs, applications, and opportunities driving industrial growth. Over two days, participants will explore emerging trends, advanced materials, large-scale production, prototyping, and sustainable practices through keynotes, technical sessions, panels, and networking — fostering collaboration and accelerating the adoption of transformative technologies across Asia.

Winnovation Techfest is a national competition that aims to showcase the creativity and ingenuity of the makers and designers of our country through 3D printing. May it be industrial or aesthetically made, the 3D printed models will surely inspire people and other participants to push through towards excellence and innovation as our country progresses with this exciting 3D printing technology.

Winnovation Techfest 2025 will take place at Solaire Resort, Paranaque, Metro Manila on October 23, 2025 as one of the parallel sessions of ACAM 2025.
