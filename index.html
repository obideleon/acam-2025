The Asian Conference on Advanced Manufacturing is the second international conference dedicated to additive manufacturing held in the Philippines.

 

With the theme "From Prototypes to Progress: Advancing Innovation through Additive Manufacturing and AI" the two-day event brings together experts, innovators, and industry leaders to explore breakthroughs in advanced materials, large-scale production, prototyping, and sustainable practices.

 

Join keynotes, panels, and networking sessions — and be part of Asia's innovation movement.
