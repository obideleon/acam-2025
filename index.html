The Asian Conference on Advanced Manufacturing is the region's first major event dedicated to additive manufacturing.

 

With the theme "From Prototypes to Progress," the two-day event brings together experts, innovators, and industry leaders to explore breakthroughs in advanced materials, large-scale production, prototyping, and sustainable practices.

 

Join keynotes, panels, and networking sessions — and be part of Asia's innovation movement.

Winnovation Techfest 2025 is a national 3D printing competition celebrating the creativity and ingenuity of makers and designers across the country.

 

Whether industrial or aesthetic, these 3D printed creations inspire participants and audiences alike to push the boundaries of excellence and innovation.
